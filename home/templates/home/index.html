Master Calculus with Science

A learning platform built on cognitive science principles to help you truly understand and retain mathematical concepts.

🚀
How to Use

Follow the progression for best results: start with Automated Quizzes as a warm-up to test basic recall. Move on to Self-Evaluated quizzes for deeper conceptual checks (these are harder!), and finish with Practical problems. The system uses Spaced Repetition to schedule reviews exactly when you need them.

🧠
The Science

This platform is built on concepts from the book Make It Stick. We use Active Recall (retrieving info from memory), Interleaving (mixing different problem types), and Calibration (checking your actual knowledge vs. perceived knowledge) to ensure lasting mastery.

🛡️
Self-Evaluation

Many quizzes rely on Self-Evaluation. This builds intellectual honesty and metacognition. Marking an incorrect answer as "Correct" mimics progress but hinders true understanding. You are responsible for your own learning—use this feature honestly for your own good.
